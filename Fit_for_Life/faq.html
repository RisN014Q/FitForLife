FAQ

How do I schedule a session?
You can book directly on our Booking page. Choose your trainer and available dates, then confirm. A summary is carried to the payment page.
What are your pricing options?
We offer single sessions and discounted packs. Most sessions are $35; multi-session packs reduce the per-session cost.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancel or reschedule up to 12 hours before your session at no charge. Late cancellations may incur a fee.
Can I choose my trainer?
Yes. You can select a trainer during booking. Each trainer shows their current availability.
How long is a personal training session?
Standard sessions are 60 minutes. We also offer 30-minute focused sessions on request.
Do you offer group classes?
We run small-group strength and conditioning classes weekly. Check the Services page for the current schedule.
Do you provide nutrition guidance?
Yes. Our trainers provide practical nutrition advice and simple tracking to support your goals.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit/debit cards via the Payment page. Packages can be purchased there as well.
